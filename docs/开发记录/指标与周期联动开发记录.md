### 2.2 数据库周期聚合

**文件**：`lib/data/database/daos/kline_dao.dart`

#### 季线聚合SQL（示例）
```sql
SELECT
  symbol,
  'quarter' as period,
  CASE
    WHEN strftime('%m', trade_date) BETWEEN '01' AND '03'
      THEN date(date_trunc('year', trade_date), '+2 months', 'start of month', '+1 month', '-1 day')
    WHEN strftime('%m', trade_date) BETWEEN '04' AND '06'
      THEN date(date_trunc('year', trade_date), '+5 months', 'start of month', '+1 month', '-1 day')
    WHEN strftime('%m', trade_date) BETWEEN '07' AND '09'
      THEN date(date_trunc('year', trade_date), '+8 months', 'start of month', '+1 month', '-1 day')
    ELSE date(date_trunc('year', trade_date), '+11 months', 'start of month', '+1 month', '-1 day')
  END as trade_date,
  FIRST_VALUE(open) OVER (...) as open,
  MAX(high) OVER (...) as high,
  MIN(low) OVER (...) as low,
  LAST_VALUE(close) OVER (...) as close,
  SUM(volume) OVER (...) as volume,
  SUM(amount) OVER (...) as amount
FROM kline_data
WHERE symbol = ? AND period = 'day'
```
